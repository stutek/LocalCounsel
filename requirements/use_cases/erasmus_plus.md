# Use Case: Erasmus+ Project Management

## 1. Scope
Contract and execution management specifically for Erasmus+ projects.

## 2. Goals
- Review project documents (e.g., periodic reports, financial statements).
- Ensure strict compliance with Erasmus+ guidelines and reporting requirements.
- Track project execution against specific contract milestones.

## 3. Specific Compliance Checks
*(To be expanded incrementally as the project progresses)*
- Ensure budget categories adhere to Erasmus+ funding rules.
- Verify that submitted deliverables align with the original grant agreement.
- Highlight any missing information required by Erasmus+ compliance standards.
